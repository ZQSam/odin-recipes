New York Steak

This New York Steak recipe is restaurant quality but in the comforts of your own home, and at a fraction of the cost. This steak will save you time and money by making it at home.

Ingredient

  • New York Steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Fresh Rosemary and Thyme Sprigs

Steps

  1. Prepare the Steak

    2. Let the steaks rest for 30 minutes to come to room temperature. Salt and pepper to taste.

  2. Saut√©

    3. In a medium sized skillet over medium high heat at the olive oil. Once it starts to smoke add the steaks to the skillet. Sear on all of the sides until they have a golden brown crust. Add the butter and let melt in the skillet. Add in the garlic and fresh sprigs.

  3. Cook Until Done

    4. Reduce the heat to medium and let them cook until they reach the desired internal temperature. Spoon the butter over the steaks while they are cooking.
